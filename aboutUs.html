About FLOW

At FLOW, we are dedicated to facilitating the transition to global Smart Cities and envisioning a cleaner, greener future. Based in Vancouver, BC, our small yet dynamic team of programmers values the importance of building a strong, secure community.

We proudly introduce: "Flow Charging Solutions." Our web-based mobile app is designed to enhance the efficiency and convenience of electric charging stations in your city and community. FLOW ensures a faster and more efficient charging experience for electric vehicle owners by utilizing built-in algorithms and real-time data, prioritizing vehicles with the highest charging needs.

Choose FLOW for a swifter, smarter charging experience.
